**Create a personalized sales report for the products in your WooCommerce store, complete with options to toggle sorting.**

### Features & Benefits

- **One-click generation and sharing** – Instantly view or download your reports with a single click.
- **Date range sorting** – Utilize built-in presets or set custom start and end dates.
- **Order status filtering** – Include or exclude transactions based on their status.
- **Product-focused reporting** – Generate reports for your entire store, individual products, or groups of products.
- **Group variations together** – Display all variations of a product as a single line item.
- **Customize display order** – Sort by product ID, quantity sold, or total sales.
- **Flexible reporting fields** – Select which fields to include in your report.
- **Exclude free products** – Omit free products from your report.
- **Limit product inclusion** – Set a cap on the number of products to include in the report.
- **CSV export option** – Export your reports in the versatile CSV format for compatibility with any spreadsheet application.

== Installation ==

1. Click "Plugins" > "Add New" in the WordPress admin menu.
2. Search for "Product Sales Analytics Report".
3. Click "Install Now".
4. Click "Activate Plugin".
